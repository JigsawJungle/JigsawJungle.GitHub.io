Influences

The most fascinating development in modern times is the advent of artifical intelligence (AI). It's a tool that can help mankind better healthcare, economy and better opponents in video games.

But like most tools, AI isn't perfect by any means. It will require more years of development until it can be used in large-scale applications such as driverless cars or in brain surgery. I want to be on the forefront of helping to develop new projects that can use AI for humankind's betterment.

Computer graphic images (or known as CGI) transform movies, video games, or even presentations into eye-opening events. The beauty given by CGI renders people speechless at times. I love going movies and escaping into a world built by CGI, and I have seen a few art exhibits that use CGI to impress viewers.

Video games have been a part of my life since I was 2 years old, and I continually find it to be an enjoyable passtime for myself. Modern video games (such as Cyberpunk 2077) are unique events where the player can make-believe they are on a journey that can change a world. I like exploratory, Japanese RPGs, or stealth games for my preferred categories. Video games have made an impact in the last 30 years in society with multi-player games found online, where people can connect with like-minded individuals and forge friendships.
